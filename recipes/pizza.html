Easy Homemade Pizza Doughs

Savory pizza

An easy way to have a pizza.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230 degrees C.
  2. Mix the yeast and sugar with water. Rest the dough for 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture.
  4. Turn dough into a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  5. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
